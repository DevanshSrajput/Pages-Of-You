The Diary That Found Me,

It was a chilly February evening when I stepped into a small, tucked-away bookstore,
The kind that smelled of old paper and forgotten stories.
I wasn’t looking for anything in particular, just a way to pass the time.
But then, in a pile of secondhand books, I found it—an old leather-bound diary.
No title, no name. Just worn-out pages filled with someone’s deepest thoughts.
